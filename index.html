03 / About Me

I’m P Ananthapadmanabhan Nair — a designer, developer, and lifelong learner who believes that design isn’t just about how things look, but how they make people feel.

I’ve always been fascinated by the space where logic meets emotion — where code can create art, and design can express intelligence.

Beyond work, I spend a lot of time sketching, tinkering with new tools. I’m the kind of person who notices micro-interactions in everyday life — the way a door handle feels, the pause in a conversation.

At heart, I’m just someone who loves making things — things that mean something, feel honest, and bring a bit of clarity to the noise.
